Return & Refund Policy

  1. If you wish to return a product, please contact us within 8 days of receiving the item. Returns are accepted only in the following cases:
    • Return at the customer's convenience (limited to unused and unopened items)
    • Damaged or defective products
    • Wrong item delivered (incorrect shipment)
    * In all cases, please return the product in the same condition as received (including packaging).
  2. If more than 8 days have passed since receiving the product, we cannot accept returns for any reason, including defective or incorrectly shipped items. Please check the contents of your order promptly upon arrival.

    Please note that the return address is not our store location but a dedicated warehouse (details will be provided separately). Items sent to our store address cannot be accepted, so please ensure you send returns to the warehouse address we provide.
  3. We do not offer exchanges. All issues are handled through returns and refunds. Thank you for your understanding.
  4. If you wish to make a return, please contact us first via our inquiry form or email. We may be unable to process returns for items sent back without prior notice.
    * We do not accept inquiries by phone or fax. Thank you for your understanding.
  5. Returns and refunds are not available in the following cases:
    • Used or opened products
    • Products damaged, broken, or soiled while in the customer's possession
    • Sale items, lucky bags, and other special sale products
    • Products returned without prior notice
  6. Refunds will be processed within 3 business days after we confirm the returned product, to the designated account or according to the original payment method.

Regarding Damaged or Defective Products

If you find any damage upon receiving the product, please contact the shipping carrier and file a damage report. Once we receive a report from the carrier, we will check stock availability and arrange a replacement or refund.

  • If you return the product directly to us without first contacting the carrier, we may be unable to process your claim.
  • Please keep all packaging materials and the product itself until the carrier completes their inspection.
  • Having photos of the damage ready in advance will help expedite the verification and resolution process.

Understanding Product Variations

Due to the nature of the materials and manufacturing processes, products sold at our store may exhibit individual variations in color, shape, texture, and size. These are not product defects but are inherent characteristics and unique qualities of each item. Therefore, we do not accept returns or exchanges based on individual product variations. Please acknowledge this before placing your order.
